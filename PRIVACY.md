# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 15, 2026**

Welcome to DevRoast AI. Your privacy is critically important to us.

## 1. Information We Collect
We only collect information about you if we have a reason to do so—for example, to provide our services, to communicate with you, or to make our services better.

- **Account Information**: If you sign up for an account via GitHub or Google, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture.
- **GitHub Data**: We access your public GitHub profile and repository metadata (commits, languages, activity) to perform our roasts and analysis. We do NOT store your source code.
- **AI Interactions**: Any code snippets you provide for review or chats with our AI Mentor are processed by our AI engines (Groq/SambaNova) but are not used for training.

## 2. How We Use Information
We use the information we collect to:
- Generate brutally honest code roasts and performance metrics.
- Provide personalized AI mentorship sessions.
- Maintain and improve the DevRoast AI platform.

## 3. Sharing of Information
We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in limited circumstances:
- **Service Providers**: We use vendors like Vercel (hosting), Neon (database), and Cloudinary (image storage).
- **AI Engines**: Metrics are sent to Groq and SambaNova for processing.

## 4. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access.

## 5. Your Choices
You can delete your account and associated analysis data at any time through the settings menu.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub.
